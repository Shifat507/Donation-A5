Important FAQ

What causes are you collecting donations for?

We are currently raising funds for two critical causes: flood relief in Noakhali and Feni, and support for the quota movement in Bangladesh. Donations will go directly towards providing essential aid, such as food, clean water, medical supplies, and infrastructure support for flood victims, as well as advocacy and support for students involved in the quota reform movement.

How can I ensure that my donation is being used for the intended purpose?

Transparency is our priority. We regularly update our donors with reports and documentation showing where and how the funds are being allocated. Additionally, our website features live updates and stories from the ground to ensure full transparency.

Can I choose which cause my donation supports?

Yes, you can specify whether your donation should go towards flood relief efforts in Noakhali and Feni or the quota movement in Bangladesh. During the donation process, there is an option to select your preferred cause.

Is there a minimum donation amount?

No, every contribution, no matter how small, makes a difference. We welcome donations of any size, and every amount will help those in need during these difficult times.

How can I track the progress of the donation campaigns?

You can track the progress of both campaigns on our website's "Progress Tracker" section. This section shows the total funds raised, the number of beneficiaries, and live updates from the relief efforts and quota movement activities.
